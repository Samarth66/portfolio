Academic Project

At SRM Institute of Science and Technology in Chennai, from January to May 2020, I and my teamate pioneered a face liveness detection application using Python. This state-of-the-art system is adept at distinguishing a genuine, live face from a deceptive reproduction by leveraging the power of Machine Learning coupled with OpenCV. A unique feature of our application is its ability to track the eye-blink count and assess the lighting characteristics of each captured frame, which are vital metrics in discerning a live person from a spoofing attempt. Demonstrating its effectiveness, when tested on a diverse group of 200 individuals under varying lighting conditions, our system showcased outstanding accuracy by correctly identifying 196 subjects.
